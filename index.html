Our Story

Knowledge that transforms — not just informs.

Baseerah Institute was founded in 2016 by Shaykhah Rukayya Samsodien — a graduate of Abū Nour Institute, Damascus, and a High Court accredited Arabic–English translator.

Rooted in the classical tradition, we offer structured Islamic education for women and girls in Cape Town — from Hifth al-Qur'ān to Arabic Mastery, Fiqh, and beyond.

No prior knowledge is ever required. All are welcome, exactly where they are.
