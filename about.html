Who We Are

Female-led scholarship,
since 2016.

Baseerah Institute is a female-led Islamic institution that seeks to raise the level of female education and scholarship through the teaching and publishing of traditional Islamic texts and sciences. We offer part-time courses, short courses, and workshops throughout the year.

Founded by Shaykhah Rukayya Samsodien in response to a real gap she witnessed in Cape Town — accessible, rigorous, women-led Islamic education — Baseerah has grown from a small weekly gathering into a trusted institution serving thousands of students.

We take pride in our holistic approach: fostering an atmosphere of sisterhood alongside academic excellence. Our classes are interactive. Our community is warm. And the pursuit of knowledge here is understood as a collective act of worship.

What We Stand For

The values we build on

📚

Traditional Scholarship

We teach from authentic, well-known primer texts in the fundamental Islamic sciences — not reformulated or diluted. The classics, made accessible.

🤝

Sisterhood First

Every class is a halaqah as much as a lesson. We cultivate togetherness, mutual encouragement, and a safe space where women grow together.

🌱

No Prerequisites

No prior Islamic studies required for any course. We meet students exactly where they are — whether complete beginners or those deepening existing knowledge.

🎓

Academic Excellence

We hold ourselves to a high standard — structured curricula, qualified teachers, and a commitment to genuine understanding, not surface-level familiarity.

💬

Interactive Learning

Questions are welcomed — expected, even. Our classes thrive on contribution, discussion, and the kind of engaged curiosity that makes knowledge stick.
